# Esar Cars - Business Requirements

## Project Overview

Esar Cars is a multilingual car rental marketplace that connects car owners with renters through a secure online platform.

---

## Objectives

- Simplify car rental process.
- Allow owners to publish and manage cars.
- Enable secure online booking.
- Support online payments.
- Provide multilingual experience.

---

## User Roles

### Guest

- Browse cars
- Read blog
- Register account

### Renter

- Search cars
- Book cars
- Pay online
- Review cars
- Chat with owners

### Owner

- Add cars
- Manage pricing
- Approve bookings
- Upload documents

### Admin

- Manage users
- Manage cars
- Manage blog
- Handle complaints

---

## Project Scope

### Included

- Authentication
- Car Management
- Booking
- Payment
- Reviews
- Chat
- Blog

### Excluded

- Selling Cars
- Insurance Management
- Vehicle Maintenance
